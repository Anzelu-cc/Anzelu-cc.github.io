Beginner Tips

  • Start slow and just try to focus on clean chords before you work on how fast you're going.
  • Practice for around 15–30 daily to have consistency.
  • Use YouTube or apps to learn your finger placement.
  • Record yourself to show progress and look for your mistakes.
