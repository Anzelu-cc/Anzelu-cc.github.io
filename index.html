How to Embed a Video into Your Website

Follow these simple steps to successfully embed a service-hosted video into your webpage!

Step 1: Choose a Video with Embed Permissions

Go to YouTube and find a video you want to embed. Make sure it allows embedding!

Step 2: Click the "Share" Button

Click the Share button located below the video.

Step 3: Select "Embed"

From the share options, click on Embed.

Step 4: Copy the Embed Code

Click Copy to copy the generated HTML embed code.

Step 5: Paste the Embed Code into Your HTML

Paste the code inside the <body> of your HTML file where you want the video to appear.

Step 6: Style Your Video

Use CSS to make your video responsive and centered on the page.

Step 7: Save and View

Save your files and open your page in a browser to check that the video appears correctly!
