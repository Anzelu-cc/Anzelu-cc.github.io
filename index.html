English 12: What is a common theme in Shakespeare's tragedies?
       
The downfall of a tragic hero due to a fatal flaw.
     
   
   
     
       
Geography: What is the longest river in the world?
       
The Nile River (though some argue the Amazon).
     
   
   
     
       
Economics: What is opportunity cost?
       
The value of the next best alternative you give up when making a choice.
     
   
   
     
       
Web Design: What does HTML stand for?
       
HyperText Markup Language
     
   
   
     
       
Psychology: What is cognitive dissonance?
       
Mental stress from holding conflicting beliefs or behaviors.
     
   
   
     
       
English 12: Define "metaphor".
       
A comparison of two unlike things without using “like” or “as”.
     
   
   
     
       
Geography: What causes seasons?
       
The tilt of Earth’s axis as it orbits the sun.
     
   
   
     
       
Economics: What is inflation?
       
A general increase in prices and fall in the purchasing power of money.
