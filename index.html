What is a theme in Shakespeare's tragedies?
       
The downfall of a hero due to a flaw.
     
   
   
     
       
What is the longest river in the world?
       
The Nile River.
     
   
   
     
       
What is opportunity cost?
       
The value of the next alternative you give up when you make a choice.
     
   
   
     
       
What does HTML mean?
       
HyperText Markup Language
     
   
   
     
       
What is cognitive dissonance?
       
Mental stress from holding conflicting beliefs or behaviors.
     
   
   
     
       
Whats a metaphor.
       
Comparing two things without using “like” or “as”.
     
   
   
     
       
What causes seasons?
       
The tilt of Earth’s axis when it orbits the sun.
     
   
   
     
       
What is inflation?
       
A increase in prices and a fall in the value of money.
